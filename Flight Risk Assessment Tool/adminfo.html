Aeronautical Decision-Making

PAVE

As part of your pre-flight, use the PAVE checklist to evaluate yourself, the aircraft, the environment you'll be flying it, and whether you or someone else is pressuring you to take off.

Pilot
  • Feel OK physically, mentally?
  • Within personal minima?
  • IMSAFE
    • Illness
    • Medication
    • Stress
    • Alcohol
    • Fatigue
    • Emotion
Aircraft
  • Equipped for expected conditions (e.g., flashlight for night flight)?
  • Might INOP items affect your flight?
enVironment
  • IMC
  • turbulence
  • cross-winds
  • winds aloft
  • icing
  • Night
  • NOTAMs
External Pressures
  • Get-there-itis
  • Don't want to disappoint passengers/someone waiting at the destination
  • Plan B if you shouldn't/can't make the flight?

Making Decisions

DECIDE
  • Detect a change or hazard
  • Estimate the need to change or correct
  • Choose desired outcome
  • Identify actions
  • Do the necessary action
  • Evaluate the effect of the action
